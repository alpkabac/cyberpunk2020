- **Throw:** REF + Athletics (or GM skill) to land on target; **scatter** on a miss.
- **Blast** affects a **radius**; cover and prone/saves modify outcome.
- **Flash / dazzle:** often **blinded** for a **duration in rounds** (book values).
- **Smoke:** blocks LOS; **incendiary:** ongoing fire; **frag:** shrapnel damage in radius.
