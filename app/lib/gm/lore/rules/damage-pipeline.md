**FNFF pipeline (short):**

1. **Hit location** — d10 table or called shot (higher difficulty).
2. **Damage dice** from weapon / attack; **head** often **doubles** damage *before* armor (book).
3. Subtract **effective SP** at that location; **AP** ammo may **halve** SP first.
4. **Ablation:** when armor absorbs hurt, **SP/ablation** at that location degrades.
5. Apply **BTM** to damage that penetrates.
6. Add to **wound track**; wound state drives penalties.
7. **Stun save:** `d10 + COOL` vs stun number from wound state.
8. At high damage, **death saves / mortal wound** rules apply—use book thresholds.
