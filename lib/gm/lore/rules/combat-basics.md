- **Round length:** ~3 seconds of in-game time.
- **Initiative:** `1d10 + REF` per combatant (higher goes first; ties re-roll or GM breaks).
- **Actions:** Typically **one action** and movement per round unless a rule or GM grants more.
- **Movement (rule-of-thumb):** MA × 3 m walking, MA × 4 m running; adjust for terrain.
- **Fumbles:** Natural **1** on an attack often triggers a fumble roll or GM complication (worse with heavy/automatic weapons).
