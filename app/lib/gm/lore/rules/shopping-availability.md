- Gear has **price** and **Availability**.
- **Purchase roll:** often `1d10 + Streetdeal or Fixer` vs **Availability**, modified by **city size**, **legality**, and **wartime scarcity**.
- **Failure:** no gear, wrong item, or **heat**; **critical** failure may mean ambush or rip-off.
- **Fixers** convert cash into odds and access.
