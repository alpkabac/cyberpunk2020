- **Improvement Points (IP)** raise skills (and rarely stats) after play.
- **Referee** awards IP for **risk**, **goals achieved**, **strong roleplay**, and **teamwork**—not every line of chat.
- Book guidance: avoid dumping huge IP every session; typical **Referee ceiling** is on the order of **~6 IP per skill per session** in the text—use judgment and table tone.
- Award at **breakpoints** (end of job, session wrap); spend via sheet / `modify_skill` after the player commits IP.
