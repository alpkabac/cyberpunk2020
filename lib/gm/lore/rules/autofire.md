Autofire (GM summary aligned with the core book):

- **Three-round burst:** +3 to hit vs a single target (standard FNFF treatment).
- **Full auto:** One attack roll; for each full point the total beats the required difficulty, allocate **one** hit (often to the same target or adjacent targets—GM call).
- **Suppressive fire:** Denies or penalizes actions in a zone; burns ammunition quickly.
- Track **shots expended** every time burst or auto is used.
