- **Humanity Cost (HC)** on each piece of cyber; installation chips away **Humanity** and can drop **EMP** (often EMP ≈ Humanity÷10—follow the active character sheet).
- **Surgery codes** (e.g. Minor / Major) set risk, time, and required Medtech skill.
- **Therapy** can recover small amounts of lost Humanity over downtime.
- **Cyberpsychosis:** very low EMP means loss of empathy; stack HC carefully during play.
