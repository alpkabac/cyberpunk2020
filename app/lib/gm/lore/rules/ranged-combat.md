- **Attack:** REF + weapon skill + 1d10 + **WA** vs **range DN** for the weapon’s category and distance band.
- **Aiming:** +1 per aim action (cap typically +3) or trade aim for **called shots** (higher DN).
- **Cover:** light ≈ −2, medium ≈ −4, heavy ≈ −6, total ≈ −8 (tune to fiction).
- **ROF** interacts with **burst** and **full auto** rules (see autofire rule).
