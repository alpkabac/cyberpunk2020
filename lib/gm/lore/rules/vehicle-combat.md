- Vehicles track **SDP** (and sometimes **SP**). Damage that beats armor eats **SDP**; at **0 SDP** the vehicle is a wreck.
- **Control:** REF + Drive / Pilot + 1d10 vs DV for bad terrain, damage thresholds, or stunt moves.
- **Chases:** opposed driving/pilot rolls; narration handles separation and positioning.
- **Ram:** damage from **speed + mass**; passengers may take personal damage from crashes.
