- **To hit:** REF + Brawling / Melee / Martial Art + 1d10 vs defender dodge/parry or a set DV.
- **Martial arts:** discrete **maneuvers** (strike, block, throw, choke…) with listed modifiers.
- **Grappling:** opposed STR or grappling skill; held foes are easier to hit; breaking free costs an action.
- **Cyberweapons** use the skill that matches how they’re wielded.
