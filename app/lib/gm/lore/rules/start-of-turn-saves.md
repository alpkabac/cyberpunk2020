- **Table order:** At the **start of a combatant's turn**, any **stun recovery** roll and **ongoing death save** (mortal wound track) are resolved **in the app** before you narrate that character acting. Treat `COMBAT_TRACKER_JSON.startOfTurnSavesPendingFor` as "saves still in flight" for that id.
- **Stabilization:** While **stabilized**, ongoing **start-of-turn death saves** are suppressed until new damage removes stabilization—narration should not assume they are still rolling those saves.
- **Override requests:** If a player asks you (the AI-GM) to **override** a stun or similar mechanical outcome, acknowledge the **fiction** (pain, cyberware, drugs) but **lean on the rules** and the current sheet flags unless the human table clearly agrees to a fiat.
